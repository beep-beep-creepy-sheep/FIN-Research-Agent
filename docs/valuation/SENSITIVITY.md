# Sensitivity

Sensitivity analysis shows how outputs change under bounded assumption grids. It is not a prediction.

Relative valuation grids cover:

- Low, base, and high peer multiple ranges.
- Outlier policy disclosure.

DCF grids cover:

- Discount rate.
- Terminal growth.
- Revenue growth and FCF margin through the scenario assumptions.

Outputs include a table, base case, assumptions, and warnings. No deterministic conclusion is inferred.
